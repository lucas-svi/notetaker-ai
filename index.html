About

Notetaker AI is an innovative app for students which records lectures and transcribes them into notes and study materials to enhance learning and productivity. Our app is designed to help students improve their study habits and focus on learning by providing them with accurate and organized notes. With Notetaker AI, students can spend less time taking notes and more time understanding the material.
